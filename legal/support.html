Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my wallet?

Go to the app's welcome screen and tap "Import Wallet". Enter your 24-word recovery phrase in the correct order.

Is my seed phrase stored on servers?

No. Your seed phrase never leaves your device unencrypted. It is stored exclusively in the iOS Secure Enclave (Keychain).

What is the minimum send amount?

The minimum is the network fee (~0.00001 ZEC). You need at least the fee amount in your balance to send a transaction.

Why is my balance showing 0?

The wallet syncs with the Zcash blockchain. If you just imported a wallet, wait a moment for the sync to complete.

What is a unified address (u1...)?

A unified address accepts both shielded and transparent ZEC payments. It starts with "u1" and is the recommended address type for maximum privacy.
