Privacy Policy

Zcash Wallet · Last updated June 18, 2026
Short version: We collect no personal data. Transactions are signed server-side using your encrypted recovery phrase (never stored). Balances use a read-only key. An encrypted backup of your recovery phrase is stored on our server — only you hold the decryption key.

No Data Collection

Zcash Wallet collects zero personal data. We do not require registration, login, email address, or any account creation. There are no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, no advertising frameworks, and no tracking of any kind in this application.

Your Recovery Phrase

Your 24-word recovery phrase is stored in your device's iOS Keychain with WHEN_UNLOCKED_THIS_DEVICE_ONLY protection. When you send a transaction, the phrase is encrypted with RSA-OAEP (2048-bit) and transmitted to our signing server over HTTPS. The server decrypts it in-memory to sign the transaction and immediately discards it — it is never logged or persisted.

  • Never stored in plaintext on our servers
  • Never stored in iCloud or accessible to third parties
  • Protected by device encryption and optionally Face ID / Touch ID
  • Accessible only when the device screen is unlocked

Encrypted Backup

For wallet recovery purposes, an encrypted backup of your recovery phrase is stored on our servers. The backup is encrypted client-side with AES-256-GCM using a key generated on your device. This key is stored only in your iOS Keychain and is never transmitted to us. Our servers cannot read your recovery phrase without your device key. You can export your backup key at any time from Settings → Export Recovery Key.

Network Requests

The app communicates with the following services:

  • api.zecwallet.app — Our backend server. Used for: (1) transaction signing — your recovery phrase is transmitted encrypted and immediately discarded after use; (2) balance and history queries using your Unified Full Viewing Key (UFVK), which is read-only and cannot spend funds; (3) storing an AES-256-GCM encrypted backup of your recovery phrase (our server cannot decrypt it — only your device key can).
  • na.zec.rocks:443 — Public Zcash lightwalletd node operated by the Zcash community. Used for compact block synchronization.
  • api.kraken.com — Public Kraken exchange API. Used only to fetch the ZEC/USD market price. No wallet data is ever sent to Kraken.

On-Device Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device in iOS Keychain (protected by device encryption):

  • Your 24-word BIP39 recovery phrase
  • Your Zcash unified address
  • Your Unified Full Viewing Key (UFVK)
  • Your wallet birthday block height
  • Your biometric lock preference (on/off)

No other data is stored by this application.
