Privacy Policy

Zcash Wallet · Last updated June 7, 2026
Short version: We collect nothing. Your keys stay on your device. We have no servers that store your personal data.

No Data Collection

Zcash Wallet collects zero personal data. We do not require registration, login, email address, or any account creation. There are no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, no advertising frameworks, and no tracking of any kind in this application.

Your Keys Stay on Your Device

Your 24-word recovery phrase and all private keys are stored exclusively in your device's secure enclave — iOS Keychain on iPhone. They are:

  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Never stored in iCloud or external backups (stored with WHEN_UNLOCKED_THIS_DEVICE_ONLY flag)
  • Protected by device encryption and optionally by Face ID / Touch ID biometric authentication
  • Accessible only when the device screen is unlocked by the owner

Network Requests

The app communicates with the following services:

  • api.zecwallet.app — Our stateless relay backend. Proxies Zcash blockchain queries. No user-identifying information is stored. Your Unified Full Viewing Key (UFVK) is used to compute balances; it cannot spend funds.
  • na.zec.rocks:443 — Public Zcash lightwalletd node operated by the Zcash community. Used for compact block synchronization.
  • api.kraken.com — Public Kraken exchange API. Used only to fetch the ZEC/USD market price. No wallet data is ever sent to Kraken.

On-Device Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device in iOS Keychain (protected by device encryption):

  • Your 24-word BIP39 recovery phrase
  • Your Zcash unified address
  • Your Unified Full Viewing Key (UFVK)
  • Your wallet birthday block height
  • Your biometric lock preference (on/off)

No other data is stored by this application.

Open Source

This application's source code is open source. You can independently verify all privacy claims by reading the code. We encourage security researchers to audit our implementation.

Children

This application is intended for users 17 years of age and older (Apple App Store rating 17+). We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the application after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
